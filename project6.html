Project Overview

A collection of editorial illustrations for a literary magazine. The project required creating visual narratives that would complement written stories while standing alone as artistic pieces.

The illustrations combine traditional drawing techniques with digital refinement, creating a distinctive style that bridges classical and contemporary approaches to visual storytelling.

Deliverables

  • Cover artwork
  • Digital assets
  • Print-ready files
  • Style guide documentation

Project Details

Client: Literary Magazine
Duration: 6 weeks
Role: Illustrator
Tools: Procreate, Adobe Photoshop
