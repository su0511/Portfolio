Poster Design

Event & Exhibition Posters

2023 Event Design

Project Overview

A series of exhibition posters for a contemporary art museum. The challenge was to create designs that would capture the essence of each exhibition while maintaining a cohesive visual system.

The solution involved developing a flexible template system that could accommodate different artistic styles while ensuring strong visual impact and clear information hierarchy.

Deliverables

  • Poster design series
  • Digital display versions
  • Print specifications
  • Template system
  • Brand guidelines

Project Details

Client: Contemporary Art Museum
Duration: 4 weeks
Role: Graphic Designer
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop
