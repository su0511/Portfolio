Brand Identity

Project Overview

This comprehensive brand identity project involved creating a complete visual system for a modern technology startup. The challenge was to develop a brand that felt both innovative and trustworthy, appealing to both technical and business audiences.

The solution centered around a clean, geometric logo that could work across digital and print applications. The color palette combines deep blues with vibrant accent colors, creating a professional yet approachable brand presence.
