Product Packaging & Labels

2023 Package Design

Project Overview

Sustainable packaging design for an organic food brand. The project required creating packaging that would stand out on shelves while communicating the brand's commitment to environmental responsibility.

The design solution featured natural color palettes, eco-friendly materials, and clear product information hierarchy that appeals to health-conscious consumers.

Deliverables

  • Package structure design
  • Label and graphics system
  • Material specifications
  • Production guidelines
  • Brand application standards

Project Details

Client: Organic Foods Co.
Duration: 10 weeks
Role: Package Designer
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Dimension
